Scratch

Scratch is a great first programming language. It's very visual - a bit like clicking together building blocks. Recommended for complete beginners - you'll be making your first computer games in no time!

You can find it at scratch.mit.edu.

Try this tutorial to get you started.

Want to know how to do something? Take a look at the cheat sheets.

Example Projects

Looking at other peoples projects is a great way to get ideas for your own projects. Take a look at Scratch's featured projects.

Games

  • A basic maze game.Use this to learn how to do keyboard movement and create walls.
